Birday Stream
-------------
- Cuts the starter quest chain to just point to trader, skipping the rest.
	- Completion of quest gives a lot of exp ( 4M )
- Buff is added to the character to bump GameStage to 75.

With 4 Million Exp, you will gain about 61 levels. This will bring your total GameStage up to around 150.

To disable the initial game stage of 75, delete the buffs.xml


* Values mentioned above subject to tweaking.
